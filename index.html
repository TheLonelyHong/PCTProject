if input or output does not exists

Introduction

At this era, we know that input and output devices are crucial for a computer. Mouse, keyboard, speaker, drawing tablet ... monitors, projectors, smart TV are input devices and output devices. Input devices are a device collect data from the users, oppositely, output devices are a device to preview the data to the users. Both devices work simultaneously and created better technology to humans. What if either input or output device does not exists ? What will happen ?

Effects of input devices does not exists.

The most obvious effect will be data is not collectable by human. Humans cannot be using computer to look through information. Working industries such as restaurants, accounting firms must be relying on writing down on a paper as a records.
